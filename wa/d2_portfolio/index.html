Wiley Yankopoulos - Unit Still Photographer

ABOUT

Wiley Yankopoulos is a Unit Stills Photographer based in Boulder, CO. His work primarily focuses on people, captured through unit stills and lifestyle photography.

As a unit photographer, my job is to create strong, sharp stills that highlight the lead actors and tell just enough of the story to spark curiosity. I anticipate what the marketing team will need for promotion, always supporting that with behind-the-scenes coverage that illustrates the filmmaking process.
